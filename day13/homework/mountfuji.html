mount Fuji

Mount Fuji (富士山, Fuji-san) is the tallest mountain in Japan, a sacred cultural icon, and an active stratovolcano with an exceptionally symmetrical, snow-capped cone. Located about 100 km southwest of Tokyo on Honshu Island, it is a UNESCO World Heritage cultural site
