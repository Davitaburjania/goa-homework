Amalfi coast

The Amalfi Coast is a renowned, 50-kilometer stretch of coastline in Southern Italy, known for its stunning natural beauty, dramatic cliffside towns, and rich history. A UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1997, it attracts visitors with its picturesque landscapes, terraced lemon groves, and vibrant culture.
